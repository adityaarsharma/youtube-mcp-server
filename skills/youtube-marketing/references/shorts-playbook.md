# Shorts Playbook

## Shorts vs Long-form: Critical Differences

| Element | Long-form | Shorts |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| Hook window | 28–42s | 1–2s |
| Format | 16:9 horizontal | 9:16 vertical |
| Optimal length | 8–15 min | 30–60s |
| CTA window | 25% mark + end | Last 5s only |
| Description shown | 150 chars (preview) | 150 chars |
| Clickable links | Yes | No (under 1K subs) |
| End cards | Yes | No |
| Tags | 20 tags useful | 5–8 tags |

## The Freshness Decay Cliff

**YouTube Official, September 2025**: Shorts have a 28–30 day distribution cliff.
- Post within 7 days of long-form upload to catch algorithm momentum
- After 30 days, a Short's reach drops ~60% regardless of engagement
- Plan: one Short per long-form video, posted within 7 days

## Hook in 2 Seconds (Not 3)

Shorts scroll behavior is faster than long-form. The swipe decision happens in 1–2 seconds.
- Show the result in the first frame — before you say anything
- Text overlay in first frame: "No CSS needed" / "60 seconds" / "This changed everything"
- Do NOT start with your face talking — show the output

## Loop Rate = the Key Metric

Unlike long-form (watch %), Shorts optimize for **loop rate** (how many times the Short replays).
- End the Short in a way that makes replaying natural
- Open loop endings: "But there's one more thing..." then cut
- Cliffhanger: Show half the result, cut at the most interesting moment
- Ambiguous ending → viewers rewatch to catch what they missed

## Silent Viewing (80% Watch Muted)

Every key step needs a text overlay. Rule: if you removed all audio, could someone follow along?
- Max 5 text overlays per Short
- Under 5 words per overlay
- High contrast — white text on dark, or brand accent

## Shorts Topic Sourcing (Priority Order)

1. **Clip from long-form** — extract the result reveal or most surprising moment
2. **Standalone answer** — "The one Elementor setting most people miss"
3. **Before/after** — 15s before, 5s transition, 15s after
4. **Quick win** — something useful in under 60s that doesn't need a full tutorial
5. **Trend capture** — a trending topic that fits your niche (bento grid, dark mode, etc.)

## Post-Reform View Counting (March 2025)

YouTube now counts a Shorts view after replay, not just first watch. This means:
- Loop rate directly affects view count
- A Short with 5 loops from 100 viewers = 500 views
- Optimize for replay, not just initial watch
