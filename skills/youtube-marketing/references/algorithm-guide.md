# YouTube Algorithm Guide

## How the Algorithm Works (2025–2026)

YouTube's ranking system has two distinct surfaces:

### Surface 1 — Search
Ranks by: keyword match in title/description/tags → watch time → CTR
- Title keyword weight: Highest (first 40 chars matter most)
- Description: Indexes first 150 chars before "Show more"
- Tags: Secondary signal — confirm topic, don't drive discovery
- **Key**: If your CTR is >5% and watch time is >40%, YouTube boosts search rank

### Surface 2 — Suggested / Browse / Home
Ranks by: watch time percentage → CTR → subscriber notification open rate
- Algorithm pushes videos to viewers of similar content (not subscribers)
- Consistency signal: same topic, same format, same audience = more suggestions
- **Key**: Videos with >35% average view duration get 3x more Suggested placements (YouTube Internal, 2024)

## The Two Metrics That Matter Most

| Metric | Target | What It Controls |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| CTR | >5% | Whether YouTube shows your thumbnail to more people |
| Avg View Duration % | >35% | Whether YouTube pushes to Suggested/Browse |

**Both must be healthy.** High CTR + low retention = YouTube stops showing it (clickbait penalty).  
High retention + low CTR = great content, nobody sees it.

## Freshness

- Videos older than 2 years lose ~40% search visibility (unless they keep getting views)
- This is the "freshness gap" — if a competitor's top video is 2+ years old, a new video on the same topic can outrank it within 30–90 days
- YouTube Search favors videos that match searcher's query AND were published recently

## Shorts Algorithm (Different Rules)

- Shorts are ranked on: swipe-away rate (opposite of CTR), loop rate, likes/views ratio
- Freshness decay cliff: 28–30 days (YouTube Official, Sept 2025)
- Shorts do NOT share watch hours with long-form — they have separate performance signals
- A viral Short does NOT guarantee the algorithm pushes your long-form (separate surfaces)

## Upload Cadence Effect

- 2–3x/week: algorithm treats channel as "active" — 4x faster subscriber growth (vidIQ, 5M channels)
- 1x/week: stable but slower growth
- <1x/week: algorithm deprioritises channel in Browse feed

## Session Watch Time

YouTube values keeping viewers on YouTube, not just on your video.
- End screens linking to another of your videos = higher session watch time = algorithm boost
- Playlists keep viewers in your content — always add new videos to relevant playlists
