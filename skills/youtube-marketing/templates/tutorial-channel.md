# Channel Template — Tutorial Channel

## When to Use
Use this template when:
- No proprietary product to sell
- Revenue = AdSense + affiliate + course + sponsorships
- Goal = subscriber growth + ad revenue optimization

## Channel Constants

```
Channel name: [Your channel name]
Niche: [Primary niche — e.g., Elementor, React, Python]
Affiliate products: [List of affiliate partnerships]
Course: [Course URL if exists]
Sponsorship contact: [Email or URL]
Subscribe URL: https://www.youtube.com/c/[handle]/?sub_confirmation=1
```

## Content Strategy

- 100% broad search-driven content (no product demos)
- Target keywords with 200–2,000/mo Google volume
- Every video should stand alone — no "series" dependency
- End screen should link to 2 related videos (session watch time)

## Revenue Optimization

### Ad Revenue
- Q4 (Oct–Dec): Highest CPM. Plan more uploads, longer videos (15+ min = more mid-roll slots)
- Video length sweet spot for mid-rolls: 12–20 min
- Avoid political/controversial content (demonetization risk)

### Affiliate
- Mention affiliate products naturally — only when directly relevant
- Disclosure required: "This is an affiliate link" per FTC rules
- Best performing: hosting ($50–200/referral), premium tools ($15–50/referral)

### Course
- Build course after 25K+ subs with proven engagement
- YouTube channel is the top-of-funnel — email list is the bridge to course sale
- Always be building the email list (lead magnet in every video)

## CTA Priority
1. Subscribe (primary — for algorithm momentum)
2. Affiliate links (revenue)
3. Email list / lead magnet
4. Course (when relevant)

## Publish Schedule
- 2x/week minimum for competitive niches
- Mon–Thu, 9–11 PM in your audience's primary timezone
- Shorts: 1 per long-form, posted within 7 days
