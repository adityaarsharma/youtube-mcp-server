# Channel Template — Personal Brand

## When to Use
Use this template when:
- The channel IS the person (not a company)
- Revenue goal = consulting, coaching, course sales, speaking
- Audience = people who want to learn from this specific person's expertise

## Channel Constants

```
Creator name: [Full name]
Expertise: [1-line niche positioning]
Consulting/coaching: [URL or contact]
Course: [URL]
Newsletter: [URL]
Social links: [LinkedIn, X, etc.]
Subscribe URL: https://www.youtube.com/c/[handle]/?sub_confirmation=1
```

## Content Strategy

- Mix: 60% educational (broad niche) + 30% case studies + 10% opinion/hot takes
- Personal story hooks perform 2–3x better than pure tutorial hooks for personal brands
- Show your face in thumbnails — vidIQ: +38% CTR for personal brand channels
- Consistency in visual identity (same background, same thumbnail style) = brand recall

## Authority Building

Every video should build one of these:
- **Expertise** — "Here's how I solved X"
- **Access** — "I talked to [notable person] about..."
- **Results** — "My client went from X to Y"
- **Contrarian take** — "Everyone says X, but here's why Y"

## CTA Priority
1. Email newsletter (highest LTV connection — own your audience)
2. Consulting/coaching intake form
3. Course
4. Subscribe

## Revenue Priority
1. High-ticket consulting ($1K–10K+)
2. Course ($199–999)
3. Email newsletter → product funnel
4. Speaking engagements
5. Ad revenue (passive)
